Description

🐱 For cat lovers, who seek a relaxing experience with an interest in business game. In Idle Cat Venture, you will embark on a delightful journey of managing a charming cat fantasy. As the proud owner, your mission is to magical item and Equipment to customers.
🍵 Start by setting up your shop with perk, weapon, lysine. Attract a variety of adorable cats, each with their own unique personalities and quirks. Watch as they interact with customers, bringing joy and warmth to the fantasy atmosphere.
📈 As your cafe gains popularity, you'll have the opportunity to expand your business. Hire staff to assist with daily operations, upgrade your facilities, and introduce new menu items to keep customers coming back for more. Balance the needs of your furry residents with the demands of running a successful shop.
🎉 Participate in special events and challenges to earn rewards and unlock exclusive content. Customize your shop's decor to reflect your personal style and create a welcoming environment for cats.
🐾 Whether you're a seasoned entrepreneur or a casual gamer, Idle Cat Venture offers a relaxing and enjoyable experience filled with adorable cats, engaging gameplay, and endless opportunities for creativity. So grab a cup of tea, sit back, and let the purr-fect adventure begin!

How to play

🐾 Tap to collect coins from your shop.
🐾 Use coins to upgrade your shop and attract more customers.
🐾 Hire staff to help manage your shop and increase efficiency.
🐾 Complete quests and challenges to earn rewards and unlock new content.
🐾 Customize your shop's decor to create a unique atmosphere.
🐾 Balance the needs of your cats with the demands of running a successful business.
🐾 Participate in special events to earn exclusive items and bonuses.
