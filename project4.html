Fruit Merge Puzzle

Fruit Merge Puzzle is the game that player can collect the fruits by connecting fruits to make Triangles

Description

Let protect your garden and harvest your fruits by connecting the same fruits to make Triangles. The more triangles you make, the more fruits you collect. Use boosters to help you complete levels and unlock new gardens.
Enjoy the relaxing and fun gameplay of Fruit Merge Puzzle!
