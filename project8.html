Save to Dog

Protect the dog from the bees

Description

Easy play, just protect the dog from the bees. Create a shield to block the bees and keep the dog safe. How long can you protect the dog? Try to achieve a high score!

How to play

Create a shield to protect the dog from the bees. Drag your finger to draw a line and create a shield. The shield will block the bees and keep the dog safe. Try to protect the dog for as long as possible and achieve a high score!
