Description

🎊 Cooking Wonderful: Chef Game - Have fun cooking and serving at amazing places with special guests !!!
👆 Tap, tap, tap! Simply tap to cook, serve and explore inCooking Wonderful, one of the addictive restaurant games. Join a FREE food cooking game now, with fast - paced gameplay and simulation fun.
🤔 You should choose COOKING WONDERFUL, if: - You have a passion for cooking - You want to experience the special dishes of different cultures - You want to explore the wonderland of food - You want to become a cooking masterchef
💓 Cooking Wonderful: Chef Game is an exciting and challenging cooking game. You will need to use your cooking skills to create delicious dishes and meet the needs of your customers. You will also need to learn how to manage time and ingredients to ensure that dishes are served on time and taste good.

How to play

📝 Step 1: Take orders from the customers
You will take orders, cook and serve delicious dishes to the customers!
You need to arrange the time properly to avoid customers leaving or food being burned

🥘 Step 2: Cook yummy meals
Tap to cook and complete your mission
In this wonderland, you can cook any dishes for your guests, such as: sandwich, humburger, cake, pizza, beefsteak, hotdog, unique food, beverage, more and more

👩‍👩‍👧‍👦 Step 3: Serve guests
Once the customers are satisfied, you will earn gold and money tip from them
In addition, you can collect keys to unlock new restaurants and locations

🌎 Step 4: Explore & Learn
Start with a simple restaurant, then explore new locations, new missions
Learn about unique recipes of food from other cultures
Complete your missions and receive trophies
